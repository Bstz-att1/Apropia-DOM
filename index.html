About CIMI Game Engine

CIMI Game Engine is a revolutionary platform designed for game developers of all skill levels. Founded in 2020, our mission is to democratize game development by providing powerful, intuitive tools that bring your ideas to life.

Our engine supports multiple platforms including PC, mobile, consoles, and VR. With a focus on performance, scalability, and user experience, CIMI enables you to create games that captivate audiences worldwide.

Powerful Features

Visual Scripting

Create complex game logic without writing code. Our node-based visual scripting system makes programming accessible to everyone.

Experience stunning graphics with our advanced rendering engine. Support for PBR materials, dynamic lighting, and post-processing effects.

Deploy your games to PC, mobile, consoles, and web with a single click. Our cross-platform tools handle all the heavy lifting.

AI Integration

Incorporate advanced AI behaviors into your games. From pathfinding to procedural generation, our AI tools cover it all.
